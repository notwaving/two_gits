So you've heard the term digital nomad?

More people are working remotely than ever before. When this flexibility is also well compensated, employees have the opportunity to combine this with working in foreign countries. For 'digital nomads', Chiang Mai in Thailand is the world's top destination. Here, and in many other Thai cities, the sudden influx of Westerners who flout the visa system to live in the country - while avoiding paying tax and pricing locals out - is akin to gentrification on a global scale.





Out Of Office stands for ethical travel and work, which is why we're not going to recommend Chiang Mai as our top destination (at least for the time being). We believe that authentic travel can only be experienced when respecting local cultures. We aim to foster community-building via our forum and our word-of-mouth, hand curated newsletter. We'll let you know the latest regarding visas and transport, local events, language tips, customs, and where the best wifi spots are.


We assume...
