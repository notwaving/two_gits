So you've heard the term 'digital nomad'?

For digital nomads, Chiang Mai in Thailand is the world's top destination.

So we're not going to recommend Chiang Mai as our top destination

We assume...

... that you wish to experience local culture, not exist in a digital nomad 'bubble'

...that you're looking for short trips away from your usual place of work, not a complete life overhaul

...that you are already in a stable job and financial situation, not looking to join a 'get rich quick' scheme
